Donate Blood, Save Lives

Why Donate Blood?

Every year, millions of people need blood transfusions due to accidents, surgeries, chronic illnesses, and blood disorders such as thalassemia and anemia. A single donation can save up to three lives.

Who Can Donate?

Most healthy individuals aged 18 to 60 can donate blood. You should weigh at least 50 kg and not have any major illnesses.

Benefits of Blood Donation

  • Helps those in need of blood.
  • Stimulates the production of new blood cells.
  • Reduces harmful iron stores in your body.
  • A free health screening during donation.
